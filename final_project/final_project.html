US Guns Deaths at the Hands of Police Officers

What age are victims?

These graphs set out to provide some information regarding police-perpetrated deaths in the United States in 2015, and uses data from the a3cleanedonly2015.json file.

This graph above shows the distribution of the ages of the victims of police gun violence. We see results that one would expect: those between 20-50 years old are most commonly killed. However, some stark (and incredibly sad) findings are shown-one person under the age of 10, and numerous senior citizens were killed by police officers.
